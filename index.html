About us

This is a community of entrepreneurs at any level, whether you are just thinking about starting your first business or you are an experienced business owner. If you are an Entrepreneur looking for connections, know-how and inspiration you have come to the right place.

Networking

Build a network of partners, clients, and mentors. You will develop the connections you need for your business. Ultimately, it’s all about benefitting from each other.

Resources

Disseminate practical business development guidance for entrepreneurs & small business owners though reading material and free workshops.

Our meetings

Meetings are structured, educational, productive and inspirational. From new members introductions to our free pizza time, each step is designed to help you out.
